Spacecraft Info

Our multiple award-winning spacecraft named “Moon Phaser” will be taking passengers on the fall of 2020.

The spacecraft can hold 10 people, containing comfortable seats with luxury food options.

Moon Phaser is made out of aerospace grade aluminum and titanium alloy.

It takes around 4 days to get to the moon. Meanwhile, you get to enjoy looking out into space, or there are countless entertainment options provided on the spacecraft as well!

Our spacecraft has been certified for space travel and has had countless tests to ensure reliability and it has achieved the highest safety standard. You have our guarantee that you’re 100% safe while in our care.

We are currently working on new designs to increase efficiency and to increase maximum occupancy.
