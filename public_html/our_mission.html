Our mission

The goal of the "Moon Phaser" is to make traveling to space affordable to the less privileged. To afford the trip to space, the travelers must prove they are residents of the United States, qualify for financial aid, be a Simpson College student, or receive a scholarship that covers some or all of the rate. This trip to space is meant for educational purposes that are only provided in correspondence with Simpson College.
